Instant Pot Mac and Cheese

Description

This mac and cheese is very cheesy and is a great base for many variations.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb uncooked pasta
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated parmesan
  • 1 cup grated smoked gouda
  • 1 cup shredded white cheddar
  • 2 teaspoons truffle oil, optional
  • 1-2 teaspoons kosher salt

More things you can add: diced green chillis or jalapenos , cooked crispy bacon, chunks of blue cheese, etc.

Steps

  1. Put the pasta, broth, garlic, and onion powder into the instant pot
  2. Lock, set to sealing position and cook on high pressure for 4 minutes
  3. Quick release the pressure, you may want to cover the valve with a towel to avoid pasta water spilling everywhere
  4. Add the cream (start with 3/4 cup and add more if you want it creamier), the cheeses, truffle oil if using, and salt to taste
  5. Stir well until fully mixed. Add any additional ingredients you may want now.
  6. Serve and enjoy
